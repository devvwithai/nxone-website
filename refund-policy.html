Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Nxone Tech sells digital products that are delivered instantly via download links after successful payment.

No automatic refunds

Because our products are digital and cannot be “returned”, we generally do not offer automatic refunds once the files have been downloaded.

Exceptions

In special cases, such as:

  • You are charged but do not receive the download link
  • The file is completely corrupted or cannot be opened
  • You are accidentally charged twice for the same product

you can contact us within 7 days of purchase at support@nxone.tech with your order details. We will review the issue and may offer a replacement file or refund at our discretion.

Order cancellation

Orders cannot be cancelled after payment is completed and the download link has been sent to you.
